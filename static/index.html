Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty on all our repair services. The warranty period varies depending on the type of repair and component, typically ranging from 6 months to 2 years.

Is the diagnosis/troubleshooting free?

Initial diagnosis is free for most cases. However, if specialized equipment or extensive testing is required, there may be a nominal diagnostic fee. This will be discussed with you before any charges are applied.

How can I send my device to you?

We provide secure shipping instructions and packaging guidelines. You can either drop off your device at our service center or use our recommended shipping service for safe transport. We'll provide you with a tracking number for your peace of mind.

What is the average repair time?

Repair times vary depending on the complexity of the issue and parts availability. Simple repairs typically take 3-5 business days, while complex issues may require 1-2 weeks. We'll provide you with an estimated timeline when we receive your device.
